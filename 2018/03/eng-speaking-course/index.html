English Course for Speaking

Posted on 2018-03-22 | In English | | Views:

Small-scale (1 by 1) English class and some notes in class.

Notes

  • How would you grade(rate) English?
  • I hope you can ace it.
  • spot / position
  • weird
  • I haven’t used English for a while.
  • It seems to me that … .
  • It targets at … .
  • thought- provoking
  • dig out
  • outdated / out of date
  • 發音: aide / aid / ad=advertisement
  • air / on air = on the broadcast
  • commercial (比較影像) / advertisement (比較紙本)
  • obey / obedient 乖順的 like a sheep
  • plan to V / plan on Ving
  • I didn’t quite __(catch) you / catchy (lyrics)
  • The most “in” thing (in trend/fasion). 目前最流行的事 (省略 in trend / fasion)
  • unstoppable / unbeatable 好用形容詞
  • Writing essence: short and concise
  • way too easy (慣用語)
  • He’s solid. = He’s reliable.
  • pig’s feet 豬腳
  • in my field
  • outsider
  • insecure / not confident
  • blood type 血型
  • OCD = obsessive compulsory disorder
  • XXX enroll in = register for = sign up for
  • Previously on Friends. 前情提要 (因為 TV 用 on)
  • The popularity of this band is waning(declining).
  • in a row 連續
  • a placement agency 職業介紹所/勞工局
  • What are your strong suits(points)? strong suits / weak parts 較常用
  • I have things for you. = I have a thing for you. 對你有好感
  • They are not civilized(educated).
  • relentless effort = hard-working / pay efforts / endeavor
  • flyer = leaflet
  • keep up with
  • landload 房東
  • lease / sublease 二房東/轉租
  • tenant 房客
  • frugal
  • public servant 公務員 / undertake / pension 退休金
  • A talk back to B / A fight back 抗爭、頂嘴
  • more than ever 比以前更…
  • turnover rate 離職率
  • A have a fight with B / We fight all the time. / quarrel 口頭爭吵
  • proficient
  • pessimistic
  • You are good to go. 整裝待發
  • I always apply myself. = work hard
  • from time to time = occasionally
  • I am worried sick (about you). / I am dead(very) tired. 慣用語
  • migraine 偏頭痛 / nausea
  • I am on cloud nine. 九霄雲外
